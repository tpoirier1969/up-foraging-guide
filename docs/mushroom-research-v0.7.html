Mushroom research v0.7

This page is the repo-friendly research snapshot for the mushrooms currently in the app. It is meant to be readable on phone or desktop, and to hold the structured notes that are now also embedded into data/species.json.

Important: several entries are still intentionally marked as group or species complex rather than precise species. That is not me being lazy. That is me not pretending the data is tighter than it is.
