Plants and mushrooms are separate canonical files. Medicinal and non-edible are derived views. Rare / endangered stays separate.

Use This Guide Carefully

This guide was put together by an amateur forager, not a scientist. I made it to be a reminder of things I've known, not a one-stop app for all things foraging.

Treat all plants, especially mushrooms, as potentially inedible and dangerous. Do not eat anything until you know the species and know how to prepare it.
