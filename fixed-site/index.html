Use this guide carefully

This guide was made as a practical local reference, not a final authority. Treat unknown plants and mushrooms as unsafe until confirmed with multiple trusted sources.

Foraging mistakes can make you sick or worse, especially with mushrooms and toxic look-alikes. Confirm the exact species, edible part, season, and preparation before using anything.

Suggestions and corrections are welcome at tpoirier@nmu.edu.
